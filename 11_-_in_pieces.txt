Guitar 1 - Db Ab Db Gb Bb Eb (Brad) (Distorted w/reverb)
Guitar 2 - Db Ab Db Gb Bb Eb (Mike) (Distorted)

Not really a lot of differences between the live and studio versions, the live one is just stripped down to the usual twin-guitar format. Brad's tone is also a lot different. He doesn't really change sounds at all on the live version, he just plays the whole song with one midrange-y distorted tone. Notice that Brad shifts the last part of the first chorus down by one string to get into position for the solo, and skips the last strum to give himself a little more time to prepare for it. Once again, sorry about the alignment of the rhythm part under the solo, it's a straight 8th-note strumming pattern but I had to mess with the spacing to align it with the lead guitar part.
